Introduction

Welcome to MyGelley! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and protect your privacy when you use our gallery application.

Information We Access

MyGelley requires access to certain device features and data to provide its core functionality:

1. Storage Access (Photos, Videos, Audio)

  • Purpose: To display, organize, and manage your media files
  • What We Access: Your photos, videos, and audio files stored on your device
  • Storage: All data remains on your device only
  • Sharing: Never shared with anyone

2. Camera Permission

  • Purpose: To capture new photos and videos within the app
  • What We Access: Camera hardware for taking pictures
  • Storage: Photos/videos are saved locally on your device

3. Location Access

  • Purpose: To add location metadata (EXIF data) to photos you capture
  • What We Access: Your device's GPS coordinates when taking photos
  • Storage: Location data is embedded in photo files locally
  • Control: You can disable location access anytime in device settings

4. Biometric Authentication

  • Purpose: To secure access to your Vault (hidden photos)
  • What We Access: Device's fingerprint/face unlock system
  • Storage: Biometric data is managed by your device's OS, never by our app

How We Use Your Data

The data we access is used solely for:

  • Gallery Management: Displaying and organizing your photos and videos
  • Photo/Video Editing: Applying filters, effects, and modifications you request
  • Music Playback: Playing audio files from your device
  • Smart Features: Duplicate detection, face detection (processed locally), album organization
  • Vault Protection: Securing hidden photos with biometric authentication
  • Wallpaper Setting: Allowing you to set photos as device wallpaper
  • Widget Functionality: Displaying photos and music controls on your home screen

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Your media files and personal information are:

  • Never uploaded to our servers (we don't have any)
  • Never shared with third-party services
  • Never used for marketing or advertising
  • Never sold to anyone

The only exception is when you explicitly choose to share media through:

  • Device's built-in share menu (controlled by you)
  • Exporting edited photos/videos to your chosen location

Third-Party Services

MyGelley uses Google ML Kit for face detection. This processing happens entirely on your device:

  • ML Kit Face Detection: Used for organizing photos by faces
  • Processing: All face detection occurs locally on your device
  • No Upload: Face data never leaves your device
  • Privacy: Complies with Google's on-device ML Kit privacy standards

Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your files anytime
  • Delete: Delete any photos, videos, or files permanently
  • Export: Export and backup your media to any location
  • Permissions: Revoke app permissions anytime via device settings
  • Uninstall: Completely remove the app and all its data

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Local Storage: All data stored securely on your device
  • Vault Feature: Protected by biometric authentication
  • No Cloud Sync: Your photos stay on your device
  • Secure Permissions: Android's permission system controls access

Children's Privacy

MyGelley does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed to be a safe, offline gallery manager suitable for all ages.
